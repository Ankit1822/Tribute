Indian Armed Forces

The Indian Armed Forces are the military forces of India, dedicated to protecting the nation's sovereignty, maintaining peace, and serving the country during wars, disasters, and humanitarian missions.

Mission

Protect India's sovereignty and ensure national security on land, sea and air.

Vision

Maintain peace through strength, discipline and technological excellence.

Values

Duty, Honour, Courage, Integrity and Selfless Service.
