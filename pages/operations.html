Courage In Action

Major Military Operations

The Indian Armed Forces have carried out numerous successful military and humanitarian operations, demonstrating bravery, professionalism and dedication.

Operation Vijay

1999 • Kargil War

Successfully reclaimed strategic positions occupied during the Kargil conflict, showcasing exceptional courage and determination.

Operation Meghdoot

1984 • Siachen Glacier

Secured the Siachen Glacier, the highest battlefield in the world.

Operation Cactus

1988 • Maldives

A rapid military intervention that restored stability in the Maldives after a coup attempt.

Operation Rahat

2013 • Uttarakhand

One of India's largest disaster relief operations, rescuing thousands of civilians affected by floods.

Operation Dost

2023 • Türkiye & Syria

India provided humanitarian assistance, medical support and rescue teams after the earthquake.

UN Peacekeeping

Ongoing Mission

India has been one of the largest contributors to United Nations peacekeeping missions worldwide.

Serving Beyond Borders

The Indian Armed Forces are not only defenders of the nation but also protect lives during disasters, participate in international peacekeeping missions and provide humanitarian assistance across the world.
