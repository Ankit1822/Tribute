History

Journey Through Time

A glimpse into the major milestones that shaped the Indian Armed Forces and strengthened the nation.

1947

Independence of India

India gained independence on 15 August 1947. The Armed Forces became responsible for protecting the sovereignty of the newly independent nation.

1947–48

First Indo–Pakistan War

Indian Armed Forces defended Jammu & Kashmir against invasion after independence.

1965

Indo–Pakistan War

A major conflict in which the Armed Forces displayed exceptional courage and determination.

1971

Liberation of Bangladesh

The Indian Armed Forces played a decisive role in the creation of Bangladesh.

1999

Kargil War

Operation Vijay successfully reclaimed Indian territory occupied during the Kargil conflict.

Today

Modern Indian Armed Forces

Equipped with advanced technology, the Indian Armed Forces continue to safeguard the nation and contribute to peacekeeping and humanitarian missions.
