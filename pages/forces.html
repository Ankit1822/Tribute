Our Pride

INDIAN ARMED FORCES

The Indian Armed Forces consist of three highly professional branches working together to protect the nation's sovereignty, maintain peace, and safeguard citizens.

Indian Army

सेवा परमो धर्मः

The Indian Army protects the nation's land borders, conducts counter-terrorism operations, assists during natural disasters and ensures national security.

Indian Navy

शं नो वरुणः

The Indian Navy protects maritime borders, secures sea trade routes, and plays a vital role in humanitarian and rescue operations.

Indian Air Force

नभः स्पृशं दीप्तम्

The Indian Air Force safeguards Indian airspace, provides strategic air support and disaster relief, and responds rapidly during emergencies.

One Nation • One Mission

United in Service

Though they serve on different frontiers—land, sea, and air— the Indian Army, Indian Navy, and Indian Air Force work together with one mission: protecting the sovereignty, unity, and dignity of India.
